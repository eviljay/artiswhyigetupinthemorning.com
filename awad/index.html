AWAD

AWAD was founded by Susan Johnson Mumford in 2009. The non-profit organisation exists to faciliate business and collaborations between women art dealers in the UK, with members ranging from long-standing London art dealers to start-up dealers who are leaders in cutting-edge style pop-up art gallery operations.
Members help promote each other’s events for mutual benefit and in many cases jointly collaborate in site-specific exhibitions, art fairs and more.
