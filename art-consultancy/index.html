We maintain and build art collections for private and corporate clients. Services range from caring for  and expanding small art collections in private residences (general art enthusiasts, collectors and individuals decorating a home) to placing art in hotel lobbies and  corporate settings (from limited edition prints to bespoke signature pieces).
“I am dedicated to optimising existing art collections, and subsequently building – as well as starting from scratch – collections according to an individual’s own interests, motivations and budget.”
We offer a complimentary consultation in which we will assess your existing art collection and also discuss your requirements. We regularly work to budgets specified by clients – either per work of art or as a general guide – and we will also offer general guides of what level investment is appropriate for what you are seeking. It is important that we meet our clients prior to commencing work as establishing a rapport with clients maximises results in providing you art to suit your own sensibilities and needs. We also recommend installing newly acquired works of art.
“I found [Susan Mumford] extremely helpful when commissioning a new piece for my hotel. The whole process was very well coordinated and the end product was even more stunning than I had imagined. I would have no hesitation in recommending her work.”
-Chris Penn, Cavendish Hotel, St. James’s, London, 2010.
