Make It Pay Seminar 7th July – Offer for our readers

Posted on June 25, 2012 by Susan Mumford


The Design Trust’s Make it Pay seminar on Saturday 7th July 10.00am-4.00pm is being offered at a 15% discount to Be Smart About Art Academy subscribers.
Normal prices: £85 – £115
Please quote the Be Smart About Art discount code: BeSmartAboutArt15

Make it pay – The Design Trust’s most creative, inspiring & fun event about selling for designers and makers!
Are you afraid of selling and money?  Want to give your marketing new energy and lots of ideas? Want to learn and network with sales and marketing experts, successful designers and retailers?

Make It Pay is a specialist event for designers and makers only to learn all about selling.  In one day there will be 26 workshops, 2 competitions, and plenty of time to get 1-2-1 expert advice and feedback, and to network.
This session is for designers and makers who have just started, as well as established ones who want to learn about online selling and add more energy back into their business.  It is for sole traders, freelancers and small business owners in all design disciplines and crafts, and is practical for photographers and illustrators too.
Our very own Susan Mumford will present a session on Getting and Keeping Bespoke Commissions and will also be available for 1-2-1 advice for participants.

For more information and to book, see the Make It Pay website (and remember to quote the discount code above – exactly as it is spelled!)

Make It Pay takes place at The Hub, York Way, Kings Cross, London,
on Saturday 7 July from 10.00am – 4.00pm.
Organised by The Design Trust, a social enterprise that helps people start, run and grow their own design or craft business:www.thedesigntrust.co.uk
