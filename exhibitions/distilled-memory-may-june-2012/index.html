Distilled Memory (May – June 2012)

“Distilled Memory” took place in London from 23 May – 2 June 2012. This Go-Go Art Girls Production presented an exhibition that explored the journey of individual recollection as portrayed by contemporary international artists. Artists included Bill Komodore, Chris King, Erin Cone, Kimo Minton, Marcus McAllister, Maria Jose Concha, Mia Fernandes and Rob Tarbell. Images from the exhibition are below.
