Nicholas Sharp

Nicholas Sharp: Solicitor
Company Name: Swan Turton LLP
Years in Industry: 25+
Also Director at The Multiple Store (limited art editions)

Following a degree at the University of Bristol, Nicholas has practiced as a solicitor in a private practice, government and industry. He offers dedicated ‘in-house’ legal consultancy service to organisations that wish to find innovative and creative solutions to servicing their legal needs.

His other specialisms are the voluntary sector and the visual arts. Areas covered include, for example, general legal issues, writing and advising on contracts, intellectual property, joint ventures, data protection, employment and e-commerce.
