Gallery 3: 16 April 2012 Panel discussion on “Pop Up Shows – How to make them a success & avoiding pitfalls”. Speakers included Dan Calladine of LondonPopUps.com, Isobel Beauchamp of DegreeArt.com, Sylvie Gormezano of Picture This Productions and Will Lunn of SumarriaLunn Gallery. Moderated by Susan Mumford.

Gallery 2: 19 March 2012 Workshop on “A successful artist-gallerist relationship”, kindly hosted by Fiumano Fine Art / the Barbara Stanley Gallery. Speakers included Charlie Dutton of the Charlie Dutton Gallery and Francesca Fiumano of Fiumano Fine Art. Susan Mumford moderated the event.

Gallery 1: 27 February 2012 Workshop on “Attaining representation by a dealer/agent”, kindly hosted by Fiumano Fine Art / the Barbara Stanley Gallery. Susan Mumford presented the event.
